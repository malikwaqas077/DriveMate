DriveMate Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026
Effective date: February 3, 2026

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Introduction

DriveMate ("we," "our," or "us") is a driving school management application that helps driving schools, instructors, and students manage lessons, payments, and communications. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using DriveMate, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account or sign in, we collect:

- Name – Your display name
- Email address – Used for account creation and communication
- Phone number – Optional; used for contact and notifications
- Account type – Whether you are a school owner, instructor, or student

If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email address from those providers. We do not store your Google or Apple password.

2.2 Student Information (for instructors and school owners)

If you are an instructor or school owner, you may add and manage student records, including:

- Student name, email, and phone number
- Driving license number (optional)
- Address (optional)
- Lesson history, payment records, and balance information

2.3 School and Business Information

- School name and details
- Instructor–school relationships and fee arrangements
- Lesson schedules, cancellations, and payment records

2.4 Usage and Device Information

- Device token – For push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging)
- App preferences – Such as notification settings, theme (light/dark mode), and display preferences
- Chat messages – Messages exchanged between instructors and students within the app

2.5 Automatically Collected Information

- Log data (e.g., crash reports, performance data) may be collected by our service providers (such as Firebase/Google) to improve the app

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide the service – Manage lessons, payments, students, and school operations
- Authenticate users – Verify your identity when you sign in
- Send notifications – Lesson reminders, cancellation updates, and instructor messages (e.g., "on the way," "arrived")
- Enable chat – Allow communication between instructors and students
- Improve the app – Fix bugs, improve performance, and develop new features
- Comply with legal obligations – Where required by law

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in these situations:

- With other users of the app – Instructors see student information; students see instructor and lesson information; school owners see instructor and school data
- With service providers – We use Google Firebase (authentication, database, messaging) and related Google services. Their use of data is governed by their privacy policies
- For legal reasons – When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety
- With your consent – In other cases where you have given explicit consent

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5. Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored on Google Cloud (Firebase) servers
- We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information
- Passwords are not stored in plain text; we rely on secure authentication (e.g., Google, Apple, or Firebase Auth)
- Chat messages and other data are transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

6. Data Retention

- Account data – Retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period after deletion to support legal and operational needs
- Lesson and payment records – Retained for business and accounting purposes as required by law
- Chat messages – Retained as long as needed to provide the service
- Device tokens – Removed when you sign out or uninstall the app

You may request deletion of your personal data by contacting us (see Section 10).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- Access – Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- Correction – Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
- Deletion – Request deletion of your personal data
- Object or restrict processing – Object to certain uses of your data or request that we limit how we use it
- Data portability – Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details in Section 10. You may also manage your account and some preferences directly in the app.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

8. Children's Privacy

DriveMate is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

9. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

- Google Firebase – Authentication, database (Firestore), and push notifications (Cloud Messaging). See Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Google Sign-In – For signing in with a Google account
- Apple Sign-In – For signing in with an Apple ID. See Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

These services have their own privacy policies governing how they handle your data.
